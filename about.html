About IDSTL

Integrated Data Solutions and Technologies limited

Integrated Data Solutions and Technologies Limited is a one stop shop for all Geospatial services and solutions. We have the right solution for you whether you want to view the visual analytics of your client data and identify difficult concepts or monitor sales performance or mapping profitability, we have the right solution. You can focus on your business growth strategy while we handle the necessary geospatial intelligence for you.

We have cutting edge knowledge of data mapping, feature extraction, compilation, management of information and integrating enterprises through GIS expertise.

We provide specialized services in the following areas:

  • Our Services

    We offer the best-in-class geospatial services to organizations and individuals that require spatial data collection, analysis and visualizations.

  • Digital Mapping and Survey

    Armed with state of the art technological tools and innovations, we provide up to date mapping services which can be integrated into various platforms such as web and mobile applications or printed in hard copies.

  • Land Information Systems

    IDSTL also specializes in identifying and creating databases of parcels/plots and landed properties. We provide assistance in getting your piece of land surveyed and registered to meet regulatory requirements.

  • Business Intelligence (Geo-Location) Services

    Whether you want to view the visual analytics of your client data and identify difficult concepts or monitor sales performance or mapping profitability, we have the right solution. You can therefore focus on your business growth strategy while we handle the necessary geospatial intelligence for you.

  • Property Valuation and Rate Collections

    We offer City Authorities Real Time geospatial Revenue Mobilization Platforms and bill generation systems. Our system utilizes the immense power of Geospatial Intelligence and offer real time payment updates. Clients are able to assess their bills on web or on mobile and make payments irrespective of their location.

  • Civil Engineering Solutions

    IDSTL apply immense knowledge in data gathering and analysis to building information modelling (BIM) and computer-aided design and drafting (CADD) to provide the needed solution for simple or complex civil designs which are safer, financially and environmentally sustainable and viable.

  • Project Management/ Construction Management

    Increase efficiency, improve quality of outputs and manage facilities through efficient operations processes and regulations by standardization of work processes and information flow, use of appropriate tools and collaborative efforts of our knowledgeable staff in a harmonious sustainable environment. We apply modern techniques and available information (data) in meeting demands of each project.

  • Road, Rail, Gas, Water and Power Distribution Maps

    We apply the latest Geo-spatial expertise to model road, rail, power lines, water lines and gas pipeline routes and link them with the necessary geospatial information to aid the construction of the lines.
